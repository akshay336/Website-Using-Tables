About US

The EMRS schools are residential schools exclusively run for the Schedule Tribes category students to impart quality education to the students through English medium. These schools are affiliated to the CBSE Delhi. The schools have classes from VI to XII with the science stream for classes XI and XII. The students are selected for class VI through an Entrance Test in class V in which the students from Ashram Schools, Aided Ashram Schools, ZP Schools and any other schools can appear. The EMRS are run under the aegis of Tribal Development Dept., Govt. of Maharashtra by the Maharashtra Tribal Public School Society,Nashik (Reg No. - MH/7393/2002/Nashik/06/02/2002).

    EMRS is always striving hard for the all round development of every student under our care and bring the students to the level of main stream of the society. The school has its own school building with 11 classrooms and well equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology labs. In order to inculcate scientific temper among the students the school has developed a Discovery Mini Science Centre. The school has a playground with all national and international sports equipments where the students actively take part in the various sports and games activities. The school has a well equipped computer lab with broadband connection and the Gamified Learning tablets EDUISFUN to learn with fun. There is Karadi Path language lab for English language development of students. As this is a residential school, there are two separate hostels and the dining halls for the boys and girls with Central Kitchen facility providing quality food.
The school has prepared an Academic Calendar to regulate various activities conducted in the school. The school strongly believes that tomorrow's cultured, confident, well developed and blissful citizen is nurtured in the school. So the school conducts various Curricular, Co-curricular and Extra-curricular activities through out the year to develpoe and exposes the potentials in the students.
