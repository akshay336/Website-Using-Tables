The admission to the EMRS is through only online selection test in class VI every year for the students of class V from Aashram schools, Z.P. Schools, Private aided / unaided schools etc. and also for vacant seats in class VII to IX and XI .

     The application for the selection test is through online prosess which starts in the month of December, January every year and the online selection test is in the month of March every year, for which the advertisement is published in all leading news papers in state of Maharashtra. The Maximum intake of students in Class VI is 60 students (30 boys annd 30 girls).
